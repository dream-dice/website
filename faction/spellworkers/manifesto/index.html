Spellworkers Manifesto

Our faction predates recorded history. We are often called out to investigate cases for people all over this wonderous globe. Since we are in such high demand we only take on interesting cases. We often further the field of magic which has to lead to some of the worlds greatest break-throughs and progressed our understanding of the world further.

Quillathe

Beliefs

  • Use magic and intelect to discern fact
  • Only investigate 'interesting' cases
  • Magic is the way forward

Offences

  • Unintelligent or non magic solutions
  • Ignoring facts and truth

Skill

Avid Reader

You can read spell scrolls from any school of magic without penalty. You can also cast spells from spell scrolls of a level highter than the highest level of your known spells.
