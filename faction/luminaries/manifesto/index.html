Luminaries Manifesto

We do things with pazazz! Throw in some razzle-dazzle with a sprinkling of funk. We do things in a way that when you hear a song or see a play about the work we have done you will remember our name. We have the largest number of famous members in any faction and that number increases daily. People love us and that is what we hope to keep up going forward.

Kimiko

Beliefs

  • Getting attention is never a bad thing
  • Everything should be done with pazazz
  • Try to make everyone like and recognise them
  • Use their fame (and fortune) to help others

Offences

  • Bland or boring actions
  • Passing up an opportunity to gain fame
  • Being rude or uncouth

Skill

Crowd Pleaser

For an individual or a group of people you can use your charm and skill to try and impress them, roll a Performance Check, and if they are impressed they will be compelled to tell you the truth and follow your commands for the next hour. You can do this once per long rest.
